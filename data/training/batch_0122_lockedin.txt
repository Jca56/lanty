<player>For real, Lanty — what actually happens when a Lantern goes dark?</player>
<lanty>Right, locking in. A Lantern going dark doesn't announce itself with anything dramatic — that's the first thing to understand. There's no explosion, no immediate wave of monsters at the gate. A settlement can survive for weeks or longer through discipline, but the longer the light stays out, the more the region becomes what it always could have been without the Lantern holding it back. And the lore is clear: when that happens, heroes don't get summoned by prophecy. They're needed by reality. ...That's the part that makes the Lantern matter. Not the light itself.
