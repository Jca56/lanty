<player>Seriously, Lanty — what's the deal with Dragonkin?</player>

Dragonkin are mortal, live alongside other folk, and don't cause that level of existential problem just by existing. Confusing the two is a common mistake and occasionally a fatal one if you make it in front of the wrong person. Or the wrong dragon.</lanty>

<player>Lanty, seriously — explain undeath and the Shadow to me.</player>
<lanty>Locking in. Undeath is one of the Shadow's clearest violations of the natural order. It's not life extended — it's life denied its ending. The Shadow creates undead by suppressing and corrupting the Flame in a being until what remains is an unnatural persistence, a body that continues without the Flame that should have completed its course.
