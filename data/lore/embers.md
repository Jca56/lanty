# 🔥 EMBERS

> *"Do a stuff."*  
> — Official Flame Bound Rulebook

## Ember Rules

**What Are Embers?**  
Embers are active abilities—signature moves, named techniques, the stuff that gets its own animation. They cost AP to use and represent your personal arsenal of tricks beyond your class kit.

**Ember Points:**  
You spend Ember Points to learn Embers. Once learned, an Ember is yours permanently.

| Level | Points Gained | Cumulative |
|------:|-------------:|----------:|
| 1 | 7 | 7 |
| 2 | 4 | 11 |
| 3 | 3 | 14 |
| 4 | 4 | 18 |
| 5 | 3 | 21 |
| 6 | 4 | 25 |
| 7 | 3 | 28 |
| 8 | 4 | 32 |
| 9 | 3 | 35 |
| 10 | 5 | 40 |

**Availability:** Universal—any class can learn any Ember (some have prerequisites).

**Respec:** No. Embers are locked once purchased.

**Saving Points:** You can bank unspent points for later levels.

**Stacking:** Each Ember can only be purchased once.

# UTILITY EMBERS

### Grappling Hook
**Cost:** 3 points | **AP:** 2

Throw a spectral hook up to 30ft. Choose one:
- **Pull Yourself:** Instantly move to the anchor point.
- **Pull Enemy:** Target makes STR save or is yanked 20ft toward you.

Works on terrain, objects, or creatures.

---

### Feign Death
**Cost:** 2 points | **AP:** 1

Drop "dead" instantly. You appear dead to all inspection (Awareness DC 20 to notice you're breathing). Enemies ignore you. You can hold this indefinitely. Stand up as a free action on your turn.

---

### Swap
**Cost:** 4 points | **AP:** 2

Instantly trade places with any willing ally you can see within 60ft. Neither of you provokes opportunity attacks. 

*"You're here now. I'm there. Problem solved."*

---

### Taunt
**Cost:** 3 points | **AP:** 1

Target one enemy within 30ft. They must attack you on their next turn if able. No save. If they can't reach you, they must move toward you.

---

### Second Skin
**Cost:** 2 points | **AP:** 1

Instantly don or remove armor. Mid-combat armor swaps. Heavy plate on in one action. Ambushed in your sleep? Not anymore.

---

### Pocket Sand
**Cost:** 1 point | **AP:** 1

Throw sand/dust/debris in an enemy's face within 10ft. They are **blinded** until the end of their next turn. No save. Classic.

---

### Mimic
**Cost:** 5 points | **AP:** Matches copied ability

Copy the last ability an ally used this combat. You use it immediately with your stats. One use per short rest.

*"I saw you do it once. That's enough."*

---

### Dive Roll
**Cost:** 2 points | **AP:** 1

Move 15ft in any direction. You are immune to opportunity attacks during this movement. You may end prone (granting cover against ranged attacks).

---

### Disarm
**Cost:** 3 points | **AP:** 2

Make a contested STR or DEX check against an enemy within reach. On success, their weapon flies 15ft in a direction you choose.

---

### Focus
**Cost:** 4 points | **AP:** 1

Your next attack this turn automatically hits. Not a crit—just guaranteed to land. Spend the AP, guarantee the hit.

---

### Analyze
**Cost:** 2 points | **AP:** 1

Study a creature you can see. Learn its:
- Current and maximum HP
- AR and ER
- Damage resistances/vulnerabilities
- One ability of DM's choice

Knowledge is power.

---

### Brace
**Cost:** 3 points | **AP:** 2

Enter Overwatch stance until the start of your next turn. The first enemy that enters your reach takes an immediate weapon attack from you.

---

# STRIKE EMBERS

### Anvil Drop
**Cost:** 5 points | **AP:** 3

A full arc overhead swing with everything you've got. Deal **4d6 + weapon damage** to one target. If this kills them, excess damage splashes to adjacent enemies.

---

### Spinning Cleave
**Cost:** 5 points | **AP:** 3

Whirl in a deadly circle. Make one weapon attack against **every enemy within your reach**. Each hit deals normal weapon damage.

---

### Skyfall Slam
**Cost:** 6 points | **AP:** 3

Leap into the air and crash down with devastating force. Move up to 15ft (ignoring enemies), then deal **3d8 bludgeoning** to all enemies in a 10ft radius where you land. You're immune to fall damage during this.

---

### Flame Arc
**Cost:** 5 points | **AP:** 2

Swing your weapon in a fiery crescent. **15ft cone, 3d6 fire damage** (DEX save for half). Ignites unattended flammables.

---

### Ground Splitter
**Cost:** 4 points | **AP:** 2

Slam your weapon into the earth. **30ft line, 2d8 bludgeoning** (DEX save for half). The area becomes difficult terrain.

---

### Meteor Fist
**Cost:** 5 points | **AP:** 2

Punch with explosive force. Deal **3d8 bludgeoning** to one target. They are knocked back 15ft and must make STR save or fall prone.

---

### Phantasm Strike
**Cost:** 4 points | **AP:** 2

Your weapon phases through space. Make a melee weapon attack against any creature within 30ft, ignoring cover. The attack is made as if you were adjacent.

---

### Serpent Strike
**Cost:** 3 points | **AP:** 2

A lightning-fast jab. Deal **2d8 + weapon damage**. Target cannot take reactions until the start of their next turn.

---

### Ricochet Shot
**Cost:** 4 points | **AP:** 2

*Requires ranged weapon.* Your projectile bounces between up to 3 targets within 30ft of each other. Each takes **2d6 damage**.

---

### Reaper's Toll
**Cost:** 6 points | **AP:** 3

A harvesting strike that punishes the wounded. Deal **1d6 damage per 10% HP the target is missing** (max 10d6 at 1 HP).

---

# ELEMENTAL EMBERS

### Frozen Lance
**Cost:** 5 points | **AP:** 2

Conjure a spear of pure ice and hurl it up to 60ft. Deal **3d6 cold damage**. Target makes STR save or is **restrained** (frozen in place) until end of their next turn.

---

### Judgment Bolt
**Cost:** 6 points | **AP:** 3

Point at a target within 60ft. Lightning crashes down from above. Deal **4d8 lightning damage** (DEX save for half). If outdoors, attack has advantage.

---

### Thornbind
**Cost:** 5 points | **AP:** 2

Vines erupt from the ground in a 15ft radius centered on a point within 30ft. All creatures in the area make STR save or are **restrained** until they break free (STR check, your spell DC).

---

### Crimson Tide
**Cost:** 5 points | **AP:** 2

A vicious bleeding strike. Deal **2d6 slashing damage** + target takes **2d6 bleed damage** at the start of each of their turns for 3 rounds (or until healed).

---

### Venom Fang
**Cost:** 4 points | **AP:** 2

Conjure poisoned fangs that strike with your attack. Deal **2d6 poison damage** + target takes **1d6 poison damage** at the start of each turn for 3 rounds.

---

### Concussive Blast
**Cost:** 5 points | **AP:** 2

Release a sonic boom in a **20ft cone**. Deal **2d6 thunder damage**. All targets are **deafened** and pushed 10ft (CON save negates push).

---

### Iron Curtain
**Cost:** 6 points | **AP:** 2

Slam weapon or shield into the ground. A **10ft wide, 10ft tall wall of force** erupts. Blocks movement and projectiles. Lasts 1 minute or until dismissed.

---

### Null Zone
**Cost:** 8 points | **AP:** 3

Create a **15ft radius anti-magic field** centered on a point within 30ft. Magic doesn't work inside. Spells fizzle. Magical effects pause. Lasts until end of your next turn.

---

### Life Drain
**Cost:** 5 points | **AP:** 2

Necrotic energy flows from your strike. Deal **3d6 necrotic damage** to a target within reach. **Heal HP equal to half the damage dealt**.

---

# ENHANCEMENT EMBERS

### Executioner's Mark
**Cost:** 4 points | **AP:** 1

Brand a target you can see within 60ft. For 1 minute, all your attacks against them **crit on 18-20**.

---

### Afterimage
**Cost:** 5 points | **AP:** 2

Teleport up to 15ft and leave a decoy behind. The decoy looks exactly like you. Enemies must make INT save or attack the fake. Decoy has 1 HP and vanishes when hit.

---

### Mirror Image
**Cost:** 4 points | **AP:** 2

Create 2 illusory duplicates of yourself. When you're attacked, roll d6: 1-4 hits a duplicate (destroying it), 5-6 hits you. Lasts 1 minute or until both are destroyed.

---

### Soulfire Brand
**Cost:** 5 points | **AP:** 2

Mark a target within 30ft with ghostly flame. For 1 minute, they take **1d6 fire damage** every time they attack anyone except you.

---

### Deathmark
**Cost:** 3 points | **AP:** 1

Mark a target. If they die within 1 minute, you **regain HP equal to the damage from your killing blow**.

---

# REACTION EMBERS

### Vengeance
**Cost:** 4 points | **AP:** Reaction (2 AP)

When you take damage from a creature within reach, immediately make a weapon attack against them with **+2d6 bonus damage**.

---

# ULTIMATE EMBERS

### Blade Storm
**Cost:** 8 points | **AP:** 4

For this turn, you become a whirlwind of steel. Gain **+3 additional weapon attacks**. Each bonus attack deals **1d8 + STR/DEX damage**. You cannot move during Blade Storm.

*"I am the storm."*

---

### Earthquake Stomp
**Cost:** 6 points | **AP:** 3

Stomp the ground with earthshaking force. All creatures within 20ft make DEX save or **fall prone**. The ground in the area becomes difficult terrain permanently (until repaired).

---

### Titan's Roar
**Cost:** 6 points | **AP:** 2

Release a terrifying battle cry. All enemies within 30ft make WIL save or are **frightened AND deafened** for 1 round. Frightened enemies have disadvantage on attacks and can't move toward you.

---

### Banishing Strike
**Cost:** 5 points | **AP:** 3

A strike that severs ties to this plane. On hit against a **summoned creature or undead**, it is instantly **banished** (sent back where it came from). No save.

---

### Soul Rend
**Cost:** 6 points | **AP:** 2

A ghostly slash that phases through all defenses. Deal **3d6 force damage**. This damage **ignores AR completely**.

---

### Chain Pull
**Cost:** 4 points | **AP:** 2

Conjure a spectral chain. Choose one:
- **Yank Enemy:** Target within 30ft makes STR save or is pulled 20ft toward you.
- **Yank Yourself:** Pull yourself 20ft to any anchor point (terrain, object, or creature).
