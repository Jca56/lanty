# Adventuring Gear & General Goods

Most heroes carry a blade. The clever ones carry **rope, chalk, and a plan**.  
This section lists practical items commonly sold in towns, outposts, caravans, and frontier markets. Prices and weights are designed to be **playable**: reasonable, fair, and slightly generous so inventory doesn’t become its own boss fight.

**Currency:** Gold (g) only.

---

## Encumbrance (Quick Reference)

**Carrying Capacity:** STR × 15 lb

| Capacity Used | Effect |
|---|---|
| **0–80%** | No penalty |
| **80–100%** | -5 ft speed |
| **Over 100%** | -10 ft speed, disadvantage on DEX |
| **Over 150%** | Cannot move |
